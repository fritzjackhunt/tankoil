OFF-SHORE FLOATING STORAGE

FLOATING STORAGE & OFFLOADING SOLUTIONS

A Floating Storage and Offloading (FSO) unit is a floating storage vessel used by the oil and gas industry for the storage of hydrocarbons. An FSO is designed to receive hydrocarbons produced from nearby platforms or subsea templates and store oil product until it can be offloaded into a tanker or, less frequently, transported through a pipeline.
